Help for those experiencing stalking, harassment, or groping

Steps to Take if Harassed in a Gym

  • Trust your instincts – if something feels wrong, it likely is.
  • Move to a safer location or closer to staff members.
  • Report the behaviour immediately to gym staff or management.
  • Document the incident – note the date, time, and description.
  • If safe, take a photo or video for evidence.
  • Speak to a trusted friend or support organisation.
  • Consider reporting the incident to the police, especially if it persists.
